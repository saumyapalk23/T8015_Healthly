Your intro to Health.ly

Who are we?

We are a group of DNHS students just like you. Our purpose is to help improve the environment on campus through working together to help alleviate stress and mental pressures on students throughout our school. Many students in response to the hypercompetitveness surrounding have been pushed to take on many extracurriculars and difficult academic course loads. This all in conjunction to the weird transition period that is high school and being a teenager, it can all seem very daunting at first glance. We hope to change this and one of our first steps is through this website. This website is dedicated towards linking students to each other, helping them build up a social network where they will be able to support and relate to each other. Another task we hope to accomplish is to also give resources to students to help them through any hard times ahead- such as access to suicide helplines and tips to help regulate mental health. To accomplish such a goal, we have prepared several features in this website: chats to help students more easily get in touch with like-minded friends and adults who can always offer help, articles/blogs which can either serve as a way to help reflect on own experiences as well as share with other students, planners that students can use to help better organize schoolwork and personal life, and lastly mental health sources that students are always free to explore and use

Why should you join?

Health.ly is a friendly environment where learning about one another and getting the right help you need can take place. All grades are welcome from Freshman to Seniors while getting the right tools on and off campus. The only requirement is that you are kind to the other members. Your life matters and we are here to HELP! :)

Heath.ly prides itself on a pressure free environment where students can gain knowledge they need for a future full of success. Mental health is an issue where the entire globe has as we hope to maintain a community of friends, family, and faculty members.
